About Me

Hey there :) Nice of you to stumble upon my page. For whatever reason you are here, I am honored, humbled, and hope to give you some good insight as to who I am as a person. My name is Elan David Kvitko and I was born in Brooklyn, NY. I am a 6 Foot 4 Inch tall red-head with green eyes that loves to code more than anything. When I am not coding or learning more on it, I enjoy hiking, traveling, swimming, rock climbing, meditating, playing a nice game of billiards, or a mind rattling game of chess. I have travelled all over the world, volunteering in different countries for people less fortunate and always take the opportunity to do so if I can. My favorite place in the world though is Los Angeles and so I am determined to do what I love most in the city I love most. Currently I am located in Manhattan but if all goes well and life grants me my biggest wish, I will soon be working in LA. Never will I forget however, the city that raised me and the wonderful opportunities that life has given me growing up in such a culturally diverse melting pot. I will never forget the great experiences I had here nor the beautiful people that make NYC what it is. Oh, and in case anyone is looking for a cheat meal, my favorite place in the city here is called Creperie NYC. They are located at 112 MacDougal St. Enjoy!

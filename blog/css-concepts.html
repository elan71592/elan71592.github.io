Margin, Border, Padding
What's the difference?

February 25th, 2016

Ok guys. This is one of the most important things you can learn in CSS...scratch that! EVERYTHING you can learn in CSS is the most important thing. It all just comes together but for this post, let's focus on some awesome things like margins, paddings, and borders. Let's begin with margins! Margins live outside of the borders. Essentially, it is the space on the outside of the border. They are the outermost part of an element that you can manipulate. Using margins, you can style the position of an element on the page. So one thing we know for sure, margins are pretty cool guys!

Moving on to borders. Borders surround the content and lay between the content and margins. Borders serve a lot of different purposes. Stylistically, they are used extremely often and I found them to be absolutely wonderful to use when working with div elements.



Soooooo, this brings us to our last part. The padding! Padding lives between the content and the borders. It is basically the space that is between the two.
